Safety Tips for Dating Events

1. Meet in Public Places

Always meet your date in a public place like a coffee shop, restaurant, or park. Avoid secluded or private areas for the first meeting.

2. Tell Someone Your Plans

Let a friend or family member know where you're going, who you're meeting, and when you plan to be home.

3. Protect Your Personal Information

Avoid sharing sensitive personal details like your home address, workplace, or financial information with someone youâ€™ve just met.

4. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off or uncomfortable, trust your gut. It's okay to leave early or cut the date short if you're not feeling safe.

5. Stay Sober

Avoid excessive drinking or using substances that impair your judgment. Stay clear-headed and in control during your date.

6. Have an Exit Strategy

Make sure you have a way to leave if the situation feels unsafe. Consider arranging your own transportation to and from the event.
